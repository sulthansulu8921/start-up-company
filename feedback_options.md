# Zero-Cost Client Feedback Strategy for NanoRays Solution

To allow your clients to submit reviews without any cost for a database or hosting, we can use one of these three professional "Zero-Budget" methods.

## Option 1: The "Auto-Dashboard" (Google Forms)
**Cost: ₹0 | Setup: 2 Mins**
This is the most reliable way. You create a form, and every review is automatically saved in an Excel-like Google Sheet.
1. Create a form at [forms.google.com](https://forms.google.com).[Error] Failed to load resource: the server responded with a status of 404 () (noise.svg, line 0)
2. Add fields: **Name**, **Role/Company**, **Rating (1-5)**, and **Your Review**.
3. We will add a "Submit Feedback" button on your site that links to this form.

## Option 2: The "Native Look" (FormSubmit.co API)
**Cost: ₹0 | Setup: 10 Mins**
We build a beautiful form directly inside your website (using your current design). When a client submits a review, you get an email instantly with all the details.
- **Pros**: It looks premium and stays on your website.
- **Cons**: You have to manually update the code to show the new reviews on the site.

## Option 3: The "Scalable App" (Firebase Firestore)
**Cost: ₹0 (Free Tier) | Setup: 30 Mins**
Since you are already on Firebase, we can use their "Firestore" database. It is free for up to 50,000 reads/day — which is perfect for an agency.
- **Pros**: Reviews can appear on your site automatically as soon as the client submits.
- **Cons**: Requires setting up Firebase rules.
